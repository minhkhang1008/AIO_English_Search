# How to get your own phrases.com api

**I already put in 4 accounts and you can switch easily if some of them ran out of Quota, however, the most reccommended method is to get your own api so that only you can use it**

## 1. Registration

Go to [https://www.phrases.com/api.php](https://www.phrases.com/api.php) and apply for an api key (write something amazing in "How will you use our API?" so that they won't suspect anything, 

## 2. Get your UID + TokenID

Usually your registration will be confirmed immediately.

Check your email, you may see a new email with the title "Your STANDS4 API Application", there, you will find your UID and TokenID

They also give you a password so that you can go to [https://www.phrases.com/api.php](https://www.phrases.com/api.php) and manage your usage


## 3. Add to AIO English website

Simply type in your UID and TokenID and click Save

Don't worry about your UID and TokenID as they will be saved locally in your browser and only you can use it
